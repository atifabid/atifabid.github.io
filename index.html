Selected Projects

  • Prompt2CAD: Neuro-Symbolic Generative Design [Code] [Demo]
    A text-to-CAD generative design system. It translates natural language prompts into executable SolidWorks geometric feature tree, ensuring 100% geometric validity for 3D CAD generation.
  • Local LLM Inference Benchmarks [Code] [Data]
    Rigorous benchmarking of Llama 3, Qwen, and gptoss20B models on consumer hardware (RTX 40-series). Evaluated vLLM, Ollama, and TensorRT-LLM to identify optimal setups for local engineering assistants.
  • Edge AI: Model Compression for Agriculture [Code]
    Implemented a 3-stage compression pipeline (Pruning, Quantization, TFLite) for RNN-based precision agriculture models, enabling real-time inference on legacy Android devices.
